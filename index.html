Discipline Everything

Discipline is action or inaction that is regulated to be in accordance with a particular system of governance. Discipline is commonly applied to regulating human and animal behavior to its society or environment it belongs.
The above defenition is a simple definition of what discipline is...
On my openion, discipline is needed from every creature to give its best possible, as without disciple, one can't even frame the path he is going. So discipline is not something out of world...
It is something that makes the world what it is today.

Why is discipline important?
Discipline is a concept which everyone is aware of, but very few truly understand the true meaning of it. It is very important for us to be disciplined on a daily basis. Discipline is the attribute that resists our urge to give in to negative behaviors and helps us to focus on all of the positive attributes. The following are the 10 most important points which explain the role of discipline in our life.
Time management,Leadership,Decision Making,Achievements,Stability and Structure,Responsibility and Control,Respect,Improves mental health,Maintain peace in society,Stay Stay Active...

All talks on discipline cannot be completed without a schedule of action.
So what is schedule?
A plan of things that will happen or of work that must be done in a planned fashion.

And How do we schedule our daily life?
1. Create a routine
2. Optimize time for different things
3. Plan your exercise and family time
4. Actually manage your time
5. Stick to the day's schedule
